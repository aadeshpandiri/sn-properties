# SN Properties - Complete Product Requirements Document

## Vision
Build a premium real estate platform for property sales and rentals with a modern public website, admin dashboard, lead management, SEO optimization, and scalable architecture.

## Business Goals
- Generate qualified leads
- Showcase properties professionally
- Allow non-technical admins to manage listings
- Support future growth and CRM integrations

## Target Users

- Property Investors
- Admins
- Sales Agents
